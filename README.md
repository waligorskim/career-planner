# Career Assistant

A passive job search plugin for people who are employed, selectively open, and don't have time for noise.

Built around a personal fact base — your career history, verified metrics, and achievement library — that every command reads from. The quality of the outputs scales directly with the quality of the files you put in.

---

## How It Works

There are two layers:

**1. Your fact base** (files you fill in once, in your workspace folder)
- `career-facts.md` — your career timeline, verified metrics, and nevers list
- `achievement-library.md` — pre-written bullets grouped by role and emphasis angle
- `target-criteria.md` — generated by `/career-assistant:target-clarity`, defines what a good opportunity looks like

**2. Commands** (slash commands you invoke on demand)

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/career-assistant:setup` | First-time setup — copies templates to your workspace |
| `/career-assistant:target-clarity` | Guided conversation to define your criteria, saves to `target-criteria.md` |
| `/career-assistant:job-scout` | Searches job boards against your criteria, returns scored shortlist |
| `/career-assistant:fit-assessor` | Paste a JD → structured go/no-go verdict with red flags and gap analysis |
| `/career-assistant:application-crafter` | Paste a JD → tailored CV variant + cover note, saved to your workspace |

---

## Getting Started

**Step 1:** Run `/career-assistant:setup`
This copies the `career-facts.md` and `achievement-library.md` templates to your workspace.

**Step 2:** Fill in both template files
Open them and fill them in carefully. The Nevers section in `career-facts.md` is not optional — it prevents the AI from hallucinating experience you don't have.

**Step 3:** Run `/career-assistant:target-clarity`
A guided conversation (~10 questions, ~10 minutes) that defines your criteria and saves them as `target-criteria.md`.

**Step 4:** Use the commands
- Run `/career-assistant:job-scout` weekly to find relevant roles
- When you spot something interesting, run `/career-assistant:fit-assessor [JD URL or paste]`
- For any role you want to pursue, run `/career-assistant:application-crafter [JD URL or paste]`

---

## Typical Weekly Flow

```
Monday:   /career-assistant:job-scout
          → Review shortlist, pick 1–2 worth looking at

Same day: /career-assistant:fit-assessor [paste JD]
          → Get go/no-go before investing more time

If Go:    /career-assistant:application-crafter [paste JD]
          → CV variant + cover note in your workspace folder
          → Human-edit both, then apply
```

---

## Design Principles

- **No hallucination**: The AI only uses achievements from your library, facts from your career-facts.md, and criteria from your target-criteria.md. It never invents.
- **Hard filters first**: If a role fails a non-negotiable, the analysis stops immediately. No false positives.
- **Signal over volume**: Job Scout returns 3–8 genuinely relevant roles, not 40 mediocre ones.
- **Passive-first**: Everything is on-demand. No alerts, no noise, no trail.

---

## Updating Your Files

Update your fact base whenever:
- You take on new responsibilities or hit new metrics worth recording
- You complete a major project
- Your criteria change (re-run `/career-assistant:target-clarity`)
- Your comp floor changes

The files are plain Markdown — edit them directly in any text editor or through Claude.
